    History of the Club

    This section of the website is dedicated to the history of the club so people can read up about how the club got to where it is today. The following text is a sample taken from the current page on the site. Dance Style - Bude Jive Club has a unique approach to teaching people to jive. We aim to preserve the 60's traditional Rock 'n' Roll jive style and to the music the era. Beginners, we encourage younger members to join the club and in order to keep their interest, we build in modern jive moves in the routines taught, This allows beginners to become more versatile and to develop routines that suit their individula ability. Taking the lead, out jove is a male lead dance and we encourage dancers to embrace the attitude and enthusiasm for Rock 'n' Roll as the did back in the 60's.
